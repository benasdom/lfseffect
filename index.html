Our Story

Crafting Beauty
Since 2000

LF's Effects began as Ladies Friend Designer Salon — a dream turned reality by Gladys Asumadu, a passionate cosmetologist, NVTI trade test holder, and T.Vet facilitator. We have grown into one of the area's most trusted beauty destinations.

Operating under the GHABA organisation (ASSI), affiliated with the Industrial and Commercial Workers Union, and proudly led by Gladys — who also serves as President of the GHABA association — LF's Effects stands for quality, community, and confidence.
